# Can Plastic Beat the Heat?

The Break Free From Plastic movement, launched in 2016, called for systemic changes to hold corporations accountable for their plastic footprint. This resonated with our interests in global plastic use which is why we decided to further investigate plastic collection across continents. Additionally, we wanted to see if plastic collection changed across regions with varying temperatures.

## Plastic Waste Collection
We use data from Break Free From Plastic’s Brand Audit (2019–2020), where volunteers collected and categorized plastic waste worldwide. We focus on total plastic counts and seven plastic types (HDPE, LDPE, PET, PP, PS, PVC, and Other) to compare patterns across countries and continents.

## Annual Mean Temperature

We included annual mean temperature data from the World Bank Climate Knowledge Portal (via Kaggle), and focused on 2019–2020 to align with the plastic dataset, using country codes to merge the datasets.

## A Guide to Our Exploration

1) How does the distribution of plastic types vary across continents?
2) Is there a relationship between mean annual temperature and total plastic collected?
